Who we are

Founded by Innovative Inc, and believing in the power of creativity to transform our society: EasyCook is an innovative solution for on-demand, interactive and personalized cooking guidance.

“Empowering the world through digital evolution”

With our recipe-centred system, you can:

  • Share your creations and discover recipes from fellow food enthusiasts.
  • Upload recipes with detailed ingredient lists, measurements, step-by-step instructions and visuals.
  • Search for specific recipes and save them for easy access to your personal favorites.
  • Interact with other users, learning from all levels of culinary expertise.
